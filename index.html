Bio

Name of organization: ” STJÄRNORNAS RESA ” Aim of organization: To introduce Swedish Stars to the world . Culture is an engine for both the development of people and society. For humans, cultures have always been an important part of life. Our creativity and sun for aesthetics and beauty means that we humans constantly create and gild our lives in different ways. Cultures must continue to flourish in society and develop so that people are uplifted and inspired. 1. Our aim is giving information to children and the young who live in Sweden about culture, music, dance and fashion contests which are hold in Europe, USA and the most developed democratic and civil countries of the world, organizing trainings, encouraging and attracting them to participate in such cultural contests. Whereas our main goal is organizing such talented children to participate in cultural festivals and contests which are hold out of country. If a child or a young participates in cultural events out of country, this may cause to enrich his outlook, to increase his self-confidence, to make friends with people from other countries, and to give them information about culture, music, dance, national costume, national traditions, etc. of his country. Via this activity we will support children and young to rouse interest toward all fields of culture and to represent their country’s culture out of country. One of aims of our organization is representing Swedish culture by young in cultural contests which are hold in different countries, in a word, to achieve international culture exchange among the young. At the same time, our aim is serving to improve the feeling of patriotism of this talented children much more. Thus, our goal is discovering talented children and the young throughout the country, keeping contact with them regularly to held cultural events and supporting these young, to gather them and to make their lives colourful. 2. We support to held different cultural events; to gather people from all age categories of society, who had worked at the culture area and who are still working here; the old generation to transfer their experience about culture area to the young generation. We intend to enrich the population of members of our organization day by day organizing music, dance, fashion, theatre, painting and chess associations. 3. Our another mission: To gather talented and creative young and parents who want to share their experience and achievements by cooperating one another regardless of their nationalities, religions and political view. STJÄRNORNAS RESA MOT SOLEN !!
